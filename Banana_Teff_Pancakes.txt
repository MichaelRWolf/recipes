* Teff Banana Pancakes

** Dry Ingredients

| 1 C   | teff flour    |
| 1/4 C | tapioca flour |
| 1 t   | baking powder |
| 1/2 t | baking soda   |
| 1/4 t | sea salt      |

** Wet Ingredients

| 1 C | warm water          |
| 2 T | ground flax meal    |
| 1   | banana, mashed well |
| 2 T | butter              |

** Sequence

1. Mix (flax + warm water).  Allow to thicken. 
2. Mix (all dry ingredients).
3. Mix (banana + butter).
4. Combine wet ingreddients
5. Fold wet ingredients into dry ingredients.
